The Fields

You stand in a vast, empty field filled with various plants, and a large tree in the center. You recognize some of the plants, actually, and you just happen to know they're edible! A tempting distraction, indeed.

Or, of course, you could always turn back.

You bend down and gather a handful of ripe berries, popping a few in your mouth as you go. Delicious! You also grab an apple off of a low-hanging branch from the tree in the middle of the field, as well. It looks quite crisp!
A perfect snack for on-the-go!
