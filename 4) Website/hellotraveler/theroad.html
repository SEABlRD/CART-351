The Road

A dirt road stretches to either side of you, one end branching into two, leading in different directions. You hear wind and the rustling of leaves down the forking path to your left, and rushing water to your right.

The split in the road brings you up short; which way do you go? There are only two options and both beckon to you, smelling of the earth and wind.
The left fork is wooded and shadowed by the thick leaves overhead.
The right fork leads to a more open space. Or go back.
