The River

The sound of running water fills your ears, the river beside you flowing over the stones on the banks. You think you spot some fish in the water.
Would you like to fish a little, or head downstream?
The way back might be bit dryer...


You fashion a spear out of a nearby branch. It's mostly straight, so it should work just fine, right? You aim carefully... draw back... and let fly. Excitedly, you wade into the water to see what you caught.
... Congrats! It's a fish. It's probably edible, right? You take it back.
